I am software engineer working in the ICT sector in various research and development activities. Currently I work as Software Test Specialist at Bitfactor Oy. During my +10 year career I have obtained experience in various R&D positions from software development and implementation to system integration and project management. I have worked in diverse set of R&D projects in various domains such:

  • Test automation for banking domain.
  • Software test automation and continuous integration.
  • Real-time information systems for Public Transportation.
  • Mobile 3G/4G network infrastructures and base stations.
  • Home automation systems and energy management.
  • Service-oriented and semantic platforms for content delivery.
  • Mobile applications.

During the last years I have worked on commercial product development projects for customers, but I have also experience from jointly-funded Tekes, Eureka and EU public research projects.

I maintain this blog as a hobby to write about interesting topics (mainly) related to software testing, development and test automation. Occasionally the blog may also cover other topics related to my hobbies and life.
